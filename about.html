About Our Platform

Built for artists and creators to showcase their work and connect with a global community.

Our Mission

To empower artists and creators by providing a vibrant platform for sharing their work, connecting with others, and inspiring creativity worldwide. -minus the noise of traditional social media.

Our Story

Founded in 2026 by a mother and daughter duo,Artjective was born out of a desire to create a space where creativity could thrive without the distractions of traditional social media. We wanted to build a platform that celebrates art in all its forms and fosters genuine connections between creators and their audiences.

Our Values

  • Creativity: We believe in the power of creativity to inspire and connect people.
  • Community: We are committed to fostering a supportive and inclusive community for artists and creators.
  • Innovation: We strive to continuously innovate and improve our platform to meet the needs of our users.
  • Privacy: We prioritize user privacy and data security in everything we do.
